6. Initial interpretation for leadership use
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
The sample suggests that Multifunctional Landscapes is being communicated not only as an environmental or biodiversity program, but as a broader transformation agenda linking landscapes, agroecological transitions, governance, investment, and evidence generation. The strongest strategic document in the sample appears to be the program framing document, while several shorter papers contribute technical or evidence-oriented support.

7. Caveats
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- This is a term-frequency based profile, not a semantic interpretation.
- Longer documents still matter more in raw counts, even after normalization is added as a second lens.
- A small number of documents can distort averages.
- Results should be interpreted as a first-pass strategic reading, not a final content evaluation.
